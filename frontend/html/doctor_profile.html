About Dr. Chen

Dr. Sophia Chen is a board-certified neurologist specializing in movement disorders and neurodegenerative diseases. With her dual background in clinical neurology and neuroscience research, she provides comprehensive care using the latest evidence-based approaches. Dr. Chen completed her medical education at Stanford University, followed by residency at UCSF and fellowship training in movement disorders. She is passionate about patient education and developing personalized treatment plans.
